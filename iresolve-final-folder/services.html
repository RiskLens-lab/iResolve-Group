Services

Separate but connected QS and PM services, supported by strong commercial and contract capability across pre-contract and post-contract phases.

QS

Client-side Quantity Surveying

Pre- and post-contract commercial services including cost planning, budgeting, estimating, procurement support, tender analysis, change control, valuation, cost reporting and client-side governance.

PM

Project Management

Dedicated client-side Project Management through delivery, including programme coordination, scope management, stakeholder control, interface management and structured reporting.

PC

Pre-contract commercial services

Budget establishment, procurement strategy, package structuring, tendering, bid analysis, negotiation support, risk allocation and contract shaping.

PO

Post-contract commercial services

Commercial administration, payment management, variations, compensation events, forecasting, contract compliance and reporting once delivery is underway.

Integrated delivery support

Where projects require both disciplines, we deliberately dovetail QS and PM into one consultancy offer so that cost, contract, programme and delivery risks are tracked together rather than separately.

  • Budget establishment and cost planning
  • Procurement and tender management
  • Contract administration and NEC4 support
  • Programme and interface management
  • Risk, change and issue reporting
  • Client governance and senior reporting
