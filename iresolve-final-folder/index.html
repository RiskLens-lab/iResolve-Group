Client-side QS and Project Management for complex infrastructure.

iResolve is a professional, RICS-regulated consultancy providing integrated client-side Quantity Surveying and Project Management services for complex infrastructure projects. We support strategic land developers and energy clients from pre-contract strategy through to delivery and handover, with one coherent view of cost, programme, contract and delivery risk.

Why the model works

We provide commercial services pre- and post contract on complex infrastructure projects, then dovetail them with a seamless project management offer during delivery.

  • Lead commercial strategy and procurement before contracts are signed
  • Carry that thinking through delivery rather than handing risk between silos
  • Create one coherent view of the client’s project risk portfolio
  • Provide clear, consistent reporting to senior stakeholders

A specialist consultancy for clients carrying real project risk

We work in the same tradition as leading programme and commercial consultancies acting as trusted advisors to landowners, developers and asset owners, but with a focused, senior and highly specialist model rather than a large generalist approach.
